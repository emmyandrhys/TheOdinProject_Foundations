Dove's Blood Ink

Description

recipe description

Ingrediants

  • 10 parts alcohol
  • 1 part gum Arabic
  • 1 part dragon's blood
  • 2 drops cinnamon oil
  • 2 drops bay oil
  • 2 drops rose oil

Steps

  1. Combine Ingredrients
  2. Steep until desired color is achieved.
  3. Inks should be made in small batches and used quickly.
  4. For short-term storage, use a dark colored glass jar.
