Dove's Blood Ink

Description

Writing with this red homemade ink is said to bring forth wishes.

Dove’s Blood ink is traditionally used for spells involving love, desire, blessings, reconciliation, friendship and loyalty. It’s also used to seal pacts and promises. Its magickal heritage stretches back to Ancient Greece when a pair of doves (Her favorite bird) would be sacrificed to Aphrodite by love petitioners. Dove’s Blood ink is attributed to Venus. Usually it is a dark pink or red ink with essences of Venus herbs (e.g. Rose, Jasmine, or Geranium).


Ingredients

  • 1 part dragon's blood
  • 1 part gum Arabic powder
  • 10 parts alcohol
  • 2 drops cinnamon oil
  • 2 drops bay oil
  • 2 drops rose oil

Steps

  1. Mix the oils with the dragon's blood in a small stain resistant bowl.
  2. Slowly mix the alchohol into the bowl. Stir well being sure not to create bubbles or foam in the mixture.
  3. Add the Arabic powder, a little at a time, mixing thoroughly, until the ink is the desired consistancy.
